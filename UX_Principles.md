# IOR UX Principles

These principles guide the design and content structure of the IOR website. Every page must adhere to these rules to ensure a consistent, high-quality user experience.

1. **No Long Paragraphs:** Content must be scannable. Limit paragraphs to a maximum of 2–3 sentences. Use bulleted lists, grids, or accordions to break up dense information.
2. **Primary CTA Above the Fold:** Every page must feature a clear, primary Call to Action (CTA) in the hero section, directing the user to the most important next step (e.g., "Apply for an Account", "Contact Us", "Find a Location").
3. **Consistent Navigation:** The primary navigation bar and mobile drawer must be identical across all pages. Users should never feel lost or disoriented.
4. **Identical Global Footer:** The footer must be exactly the same on every page, featuring 5 distinct columns: Fuel Solutions, Digital Platforms, Industries, Corporate, and Quick Actions.
5. **Scroll-Triggered Animations:** To create a modern, engaging experience, all content sections must use scroll-triggered animations (`data-animate="fade-up"` or `class="fade-up"`), managed by the global `ior-global.js` script.
6. **SEO Fundamentals:** Every page must include a unique `<title>` and `<meta name="description">` tag to ensure proper search engine indexing and display.
7. **Mobile Drawer Functionality:** The mobile hamburger menu and drawer must function flawlessly on all pages, providing access to the full site hierarchy on smaller screens.
8. **Zero Harm First:** Safety is paramount. Content related to Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) must be prominent, clear, and uncompromising.
9. **Regional-First Tone:** The language and imagery must resonate with regional Australia, reflecting the hard work and practical nature of IOR's core customer base.
10. **Visual Hierarchy:** Use typography (H1, H2, H3) and colour contrast (Primary Blue, Navy, White) to guide the user's eye to the most critical information first.
