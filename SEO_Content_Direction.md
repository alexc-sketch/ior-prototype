## How to Use This Document
- **Primary Keyword:** The single most important term this page should rank for. Use it in the `<title>`, `<h1>`, and first paragraph.
- **Secondary Keywords:** Supporting terms to weave naturally into body copy, subheadings, and image alt text.
- **Content Intent:** The user's goal when they land on this page. Content must answer this intent immediately.
- **CTA Direction:** The primary action the user should take after reading.
