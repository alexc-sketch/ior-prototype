Why the IOR Aviation Network

Built for aviation. Available wherever you fly.

24/7 Unmanned Access

Access fuel any time of day or night at regional and remote airports. Our unmanned facilities are always available — no staff required, no waiting.

JIG-Compliant Supply

Absolute quality assurance. All fuels dispensed through the IOR Aviation Network meet Australian standards and Joint Inspection Group quality requirements.

RFID Tag Technology

Account holders use their encrypted IOR RFID tag for instant access at the bowser. No cash, no cards, no PIN codes.

Customer Portal Visibility

Every transaction recorded per aircraft registration. Real-time reporting and consolidated invoicing through the IOR Customer Portal.

FAQs

IOR Aviation Network — common questions.

  • The IOR Aviation Network is a network of 30+ unmanned aviation fuel stops across Australia, providing 24/7 access to JIG-compliant Jet A-1 and Avgas 100LL. Account holders access fuel using an encrypted RFID tag. All transactions are consolidated into a single monthly invoice.
  • The IOR Aviation Network provides Jet A-1 and Avgas 100LL. Fuel availability varies by location. The IOR Aviation Network Directory and Location Finder both indicate which fuel types are available at each stop.
  • Yes. All fuels dispensed through the IOR Aviation Network meet Australian standards and Joint Inspection Group (JIG) quality requirements. IOR maintains rigorous quality assurance across all aviation fuel supply and storage.
  • Yes. Transient pilots and operators without an IOR account can pay at the bowser using the Fuelcharge app. Download the app, select your location, and pay instantly — no account, no RFID tag, no cash required.
  • Apply using the form on this page or via the Apply for an Account button. Our aviation team will review your application and be in touch to establish your account and arrange dispatch of your aircraft RFID tags.
  • Yes. IOR Aviation accounts support multiple aircraft registrations under a single account. Each aircraft is assigned its own RFID tag, and all transactions are reported per registration through the IOR Customer Portal. Both Jet A-1 and Avgas can be managed under the same account.
