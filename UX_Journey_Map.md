# IOR UX Journey Map

This document outlines the primary user personas and their intended navigation flows through the IOR website. Every page must support these journeys with clear, contextual Calls to Action (CTAs).

## 1. The Utility User (Driver / Operator)
**Goal:** Needs fast access to physical locations, the Fuelcharge app, and account applications. They are often on mobile, in a truck cab, or at a site.

**Key Flows:**
- **Find a Stop:** Homepage → Quick Actions (Find a Location) → Diesel Network Map
- **Get the App:** Homepage → Digital Platforms → Fuelcharge → Download App (Apple/Google)
- **Apply for Account:** Homepage → Apply for an Account (Header/Footer CTA) → Application Form

**UX Requirements:**
- Large, tap-friendly buttons.
- High contrast for outdoor visibility.
- Immediate access to maps and app downloads.

## 2. The Revenue Engine (Procurement / B2B Decision Maker)
**Goal:** Needs assurance of supply chain reliability, bulk delivery specifications, and direct contact with regional sales managers.

**Key Flows:**
- **Industry Specifics:** Homepage → Industries Hub → Mining & Resources (or Transport) → Contact Regional Manager
- **Bulk Supply:** Homepage → Fuel Solutions Hub → Ground Fuels → Bulk Diesel Delivery → Request Quote
- **Aviation Supply:** Homepage → Aviation Hub → Aviation Bulk Delivery → Contact Aviation Team

**UX Requirements:**
- Clear articulation of IOR's sovereign supply chain (refinery to pump).
- Easy access to technical specifications (e.g., ISO certifications, HyBlue AdBlue specs).
- Prominent "Contact Us" or "Request Quote" CTAs.

## 3. The Talent (Job Seeker)
**Goal:** Wants to understand company culture, regional benefits, and clear application pathways.

**Key Flows:**
- **Explore Culture:** Homepage → About IOR → Careers Hub → Watch EVP Videos
- **Find a Pathway:** Careers Hub → Entry Pathways (or Apprentices / Graduates) → View Open Roles
- **Regional Living:** Careers Hub → Working Regionally → Apply Now

**UX Requirements:**
- Authentic employee stories (EVP videos).
- Clear breakdown of different career stages (School-based, Apprentice, Graduate, Experienced).
- Direct links to the application portal.

## 4. The Community Stakeholder
**Goal:** Wants to understand IOR's impact on regional Australia, sponsorships, and ESG commitments.

**Key Flows:**
- **Sponsorships:** Homepage → Corporate (Footer) → Community & Giving Back → Apply for Sponsorship
- **Sustainability:** Homepage → Corporate (Footer) → Sustainability, HSE & ESG → View Certifications

**UX Requirements:**
- Highlighting key partnerships (LifeFlight, Heart of Australia).
- Clear visibility of the "Zero Harm" policy and ISO certifications.
