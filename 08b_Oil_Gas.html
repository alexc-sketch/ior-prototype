Getting Started

Remote supply in 5 business days

Tell us your site location and requirements — IOR handles the rest.

1

Submit an Enquiry

Contact IOR's oil and gas supply team. Tell us your site location, fuel types, volumes, and project timeline.

2

Site Assessment & Logistics Plan

IOR's logistics team develops a supply plan for your site. Frac tank deployment, delivery schedule, and HyDip™ monitoring configuration.

3

Infrastructure Deployment

IOR delivers and installs frac tanks and storage infrastructure. Typically within 5 business days of contract execution.
