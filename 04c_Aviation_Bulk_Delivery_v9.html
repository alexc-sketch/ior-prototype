Bulk Aviation Delivery: Common Questions.

IOR supplies Jet A-1 (turbine fuel) and Avgas 100LL (piston engine fuel) for bulk delivery. Both fuel types are sourced from Australia's largest importers and delivered in dedicated aviation-grade tankers that are never used for diesel.

Yes. IOR's aviation tanker fleet is dedicated exclusively to aviation fuels. They are never used for diesel, AdBlue, or any other ground fuel product. This eliminates the switch loading contamination risk entirely.

Yes. Our remote-capable aviation tanker fleet is equipped for unsealed roads and off-road delivery conditions. We regularly deliver to mining camp airstrips and remote pastoral stations.

Every IOR bulk aviation fuel delivery is accompanied by a batch certificate confirming the fuel meets specification, a Safety Data Sheet (SDS), and a delivery docket recording the fuel grade and volume. Digital records are available via the IOR Customer Portal.
