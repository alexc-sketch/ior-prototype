How It Works

From source to storage — certified at every step.

IOR's bulk aviation fuel delivery process is built around quality assurance and traceability. Every delivery is documented, every batch is tested, and every tank is filled to specification.

1
Enquiry & site assessment
Tell us about your facility — location, access conditions, storage capacity, fuel types required, and estimated volume. IOR will assess the right tanker configuration and delivery schedule for your operation.
2
Tailored quote & supply agreement
Our Aviation team provides a competitive bulk quote based on your volume, frequency, and location. We structure supply agreements around your operation — not the other way around.
3
Batch testing & certification
Before any fuel leaves our terminal, it is tested against Jet A-1 or Avgas specification. Batch certificates are issued and retained for full traceability. You receive documentation with every delivery.
4
Scheduled delivery to your facility
IOR coordinates delivery around your operation. We notify you in advance, track every delivery, and can integrate with HyDip™ FMS for automated reorder when your tank reaches a set level.
5
Delivery documentation & digital records
Every delivery generates a complete record — fuel grade, volume, batch certificate reference, operator ID, and timestamp. Records are available digitally via the IOR Customer Portal and provided as printed delivery dockets on request.

IOR supplies Jet A-1 (turbine fuel) and Avgas 100LL (piston engine fuel) for bulk delivery. Both fuel types are sourced from Australia's largest importers, held in IOR's own terminal network, and delivered in dedicated aviation-grade tankers that are never used for diesel or other ground fuels.
