Tin Can Bay Marina: 24/7 Fuel Access with HyDip™

Client: Tin Can Bay Marina

A regional Queensland marina needed to modernise its fuel facility and unlock 24/7 revenue. IOR delivered a complete HyDip™ solution — new tanks, new technology, and a new revenue stream.

The Challenge

Tin Can Bay Marina, situated approximately 210km north of Brisbane on the Great Sandy Strait, services 172 short and long-term berths, fishing charters, and commercial and recreational boaters. The marina's fuel facility was ageing and needed a complete renovation — but more importantly, the marina needed a solution that would allow it to sell fuel 24/7, even when the site was unmanned.

The challenge was not just infrastructure — it was enabling a new revenue model. The marina's proprietor wanted to unlock the commercial potential of 24/7 fuel access without the cost of staffing the site around the clock.

The Solution

IOR delivered a complete fuel facility upgrade, anchored by HyDip™ fuel management and payment technology. The solution enables the marina to track fuel usage, sell fuel 24/7, and allow customers to access fuel via RFID tag on account or via the Fuelcharge app — with no personnel required on site.

  • HyDip™ fuel dispensing and payment technology installed
  • New self-bunded diesel tank installed
  • Underground unleaded tank tested and recertified
  • New underground piping between tanks and dispensers installed
  • New wiring and electrical controls installed
  • New signage, placarding, and safety documentation installed
  • Fuelcharge app integration enabling flexible 24/7 customer access

Tin Can Bay Marina can now monitor fuel sales and usage through HyDip™ Cloud from any device. Customers benefit from 24/7 access to fuel even when the site is unmanned — generating revenue for the marina around the clock.

"We can now earn revenue from fuel sales even when the site is unmanned. HyDip has completely changed how we operate the marina's fuel facility."
— Chris Rippon, Proprietor, Tin Can Bay Marina
