WHY START EARLY

What you get from an IOR school program

💰

Earn While You Learn

Get paid for your time on site. IOR's school-based programs are paid work experience — not unpaid internships. You'll earn real money while building real skills.

📜

Nationally Recognised Training

Complete units from nationally recognised qualifications as part of your program. These count towards your future apprenticeship or certificate — giving you a head start before you even finish school.

🚀

Pathway to Full-Time Employment

Many of our school-based trainees go on to apprenticeships or full-time roles at IOR. We have a track record of promoting from within, with many of our leaders starting in entry-level roles.

📍

Regional & Metro Locations

Programs available across Queensland, New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia, Western Australia, and the Northern Territory — in both regional and metro locations.

🤝

Mentoring from Day One

You'll be paired with an experienced IOR team member who'll guide you through your program, answer your questions, and help you get the most out of every day on site.

🏆

Real Industry Experience

IOR is one of Australia's largest fuel infrastructure companies. Working with us gives you experience that stands out on any resume — and skills that transfer across the energy, transport, and infrastructure sectors.
