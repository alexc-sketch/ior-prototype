Indigenous Community · Giving Back

Red Dust Role Models: investing in the next generation.

IOR is a proud supporter of Red Dust Role Models — an organisation that sends professional athletes and role models into remote Indigenous communities to deliver health, education, and wellbeing programs. For IOR, this partnership is personal. Many of the communities Red Dust serves are the same communities that depend on IOR's fuel network to keep their hospitals, schools, and essential services running.

"The Break the Cycle 2018 program brought together 60 young people from the Tanami region. IOR's support made it possible." — Red Dust Role Models, Annual Report 2018

Aeromedical · Community Safety

Keeping LifeFlight in the air.

IOR is a long-standing partner of LifeFlight — Australia's largest aeromedical service. When a LifeFlight helicopter takes off to reach a patient in a remote location, IOR aviation fuel is often what makes that flight possible. This partnership is one of the most direct expressions of IOR's purpose: fuelling the services that keep Australians safe.

IOR supplies Jet A-1 aviation fuel to LifeFlight bases across Queensland, ensuring the fleet is always ready to respond — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.

Toowoomba Wellcamp IMAGE PLACEHOLDER Qantas pilot training academy, Toowoomba Wellcamp Airport, aircraft on tarmac
Regional Development · Aviation

Training the next generation of Australian pilots.

When the Qantas Group Pilot Training Academy opened at Toowoomba Wellcamp Airport in 2020, IOR was there — building a state-of-the-art refuelling facility capable of fuelling three aircraft simultaneously from a 55,000-litre tank. This project was about more than fuel. It was about IOR's commitment to regional Queensland and to the industries that will define Australia's future.

"IOR demonstrated its entrepreneurial and innovative capability to meet our needs at the Toowoomba Wellcamp Airport facility." — Flight Training Adelaide (FTA), 2020

Agriculture · Regional Australia

Standing behind Australian agriculture.

IOR is a proud AgForce partner — supporting the peak body for Queensland's rural industries. Agriculture is one of IOR's most important customer sectors, and this partnership reflects a genuine commitment to the farmers, graziers, and agribusinesses that feed Australia and the world.

Through the AgForce partnership, IOR provides preferential fuel pricing, dedicated account management, and seasonal delivery guarantees to AgForce members across Queensland and beyond.
