Community & Giving Back

We never forget where we came from.

IOR was built in regional Australia. We operate in the places others overlook — and we believe that growth should create joint value between our business and the communities that make it possible.

Our community support model is built on three pillars: direct support, social support, and economic support. We don't just fuel industries — we invest in the people and places behind them.

IOR Community Support Model

Three pillars. One commitment.

As an organisation, we seek to ensure that our growth creates joint value between our business and the community. Our approach to supporting Australian communities is structured across three pillars.

🤝
Direct Support
We seek to minimise our environmental impact and ensure our operations provide direct support to the community.
  • Bringing energy to regional Australia
  • Providing a quality, reliable service
  • Managing health and safety risks
  • Minimising our environmental impact
💛
Social Support
We build long-standing relationships and earn the trust of the communities in which we work, through targeted social investments.
  • Corporate Social Responsibility investment
  • Reconciliation Action Plan commitment
  • Equal opportunity for all who work with IOR
📈
Economic Support
Through our operations, we directly enable economic and jobs growth in the regions we serve.
  • Local employment program — talent from within communities
  • Buy local program — direct link to local suppliers
  • Engagement with AgForce and resources industry bodies
Corporate Sponsorships

Proudly supporting organisations that save lives.

Our core value of supporting and developing regional, rural and remote Australia drives us to be the major sponsor of several not-for-profit and profit-for-purpose organisations.

🚁
IMAGE PLACEHOLDER RACQ LifeFlight helicopter — night rescue, outback QLD
Major Sponsor

RACQ LifeFlight Rescue

LifeFlight is one of Australia's largest aeromedical charities, providing rapid response medical care to thousands of people each year. Queensland's rural and remote communities rely on the LifeFlight helicopters to bring the hospital to them in their darkest hour.

IOR is proud to be a major sponsor of LifeFlight — because in the places we operate, access to emergency medical care isn't a given. It's something worth fighting for.

"When minutes matter and the nearest hospital is hundreds of kilometres away, LifeFlight is the difference between life and death. IOR's support helps make that possible." — IOR Community Partnership

IMAGE PLACEHOLDER Heart of Australia road train clinic — regional Queensland
Major Sponsor

Heart of Australia

Heart of Australia delivers specialist medical services to regional, rural and remote area communities across Queensland. Using customised road trains — specialist medical clinics-on-wheels — they have seen over 5,000 patients across 150,000km on the road and helped save more than 250 lives.

The same roads IOR's fuel network runs along are the roads Heart of Australia travels to reach patients who would otherwise go without specialist care. We're proud to help keep those wheels turning.

Learn More About Heart of Australia →

IMAGE PLACEHOLDER Outback Futures mental health services — remote Queensland
Major Sponsor

Outback Futures

Outback Futures is a not-for-profit organisation committed to providing regular, professional and consistent mental and allied health and wellbeing services to rural and remote Queenslanders.

In the regions IOR serves, mental health support is often the hardest resource to access. Outback Futures changes that — and IOR is proud to support their work in the communities where we operate.

Learn More About Outback Futures →
🎗️
IMAGE PLACEHOLDER It's a Bloke Thing roadshow — regional Queensland towns
Major Sponsor

It's a Bloke Thing

It's a Bloke Thing raises funds to educate men and women in rural Australia about prostate cancer. Their roadshow visits over 65 towns throughout regional Queensland annually, covering over 25,000km. The organisation's goal is to raise public awareness around prostate cancer and its treatment.

Men in regional Australia are less likely to seek medical help. It's a Bloke Thing meets them where they are — and IOR is proud to support that mission.

Learn More →
🏄
IMAGE PLACEHOLDER Westpac Lifesaver helicopter — South East Queensland coast patrol
Major Sponsor

Westpac Lifesaver Rescue Helicopter Service QLD

One of the oldest community-based helicopter rescue services in the world, operating across South-East Queensland since 1976. The service patrols the South East Queensland coast and carries out beach surveillance, shark warnings, preventative actions, missing person searches and rescues.

IOR's support helps keep this vital service in the air — protecting the communities and coastlines of South East Queensland.

Community Sponsorships

Supporting the regions we call home.

In each of the regions in which we work, we are proud to support events, causes, and initiatives. Our support may take many forms including staff volunteering, financial contributions, or in-kind donations of fuel.

🌾
Agricultural Events
Sponsoring field days, shows, and agricultural community events across regional Queensland, NSW, SA, and WA.
⛽
In-Kind Fuel Donations
Providing fuel to community events, volunteer organisations, and emergency services in the regions we operate.
👷
Staff Volunteering
IOR team members actively volunteer in the communities where they live and work, contributing time and skills to local causes.
🤝
Reconciliation Action Plan
IOR is committed to a Reconciliation Action Plan, recognising and respecting the Traditional Owners of the lands on which we operate.
🏆
Local Employment
Our local employment program attracts talent from within the communities in which we work, creating sustainable regional jobs.
🛒
Buy Local Program
A direct link between IOR's procurement and local suppliers — keeping money in the regions that fuel our business.
