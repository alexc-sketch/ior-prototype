Our Approach

Creating Joint Value

As an organisation, we seek to ensure that our growth creates joint value between our business and the community. Our approach to supporting Australian communities is via three pillars: Direct support, Social support, and Economic support.

Our Support Model

Three pillars of community support.

01
Direct Support
Bringing energy to regional Australia, providing a quality service, managing health and safety risks, and managing our environmental impact.
02
Social Support
Corporate Social Responsibility investment, committing to a Reconciliation Action Plan, and providing all who work with IOR an equal opportunity to succeed.
03
Economic Support
Our local employment program, our buy local program, and our engagement with industry bodies that advocate for progressing agriculture and resources industries in Australia.

Our Partnerships

Keeping aeromedical helicopters in the air.

When every second counts, fuel is life. IOR is a proud sponsor of RACQ LifeFlight, Australia's largest aeromedical helicopter service. Our fuel keeps their fleet operational across Queensland, ensuring that no matter how remote the emergency, help is on its way.

Our Partnerships

Fuelling specialist healthcare in the bush.

Heart of Australia brings world-class cardiac, respiratory, and specialist care to regional Queensland communities that would otherwise go without. IOR is proud to supply the fuel that keeps the Heart buses on the road and the specialists on their rounds.
