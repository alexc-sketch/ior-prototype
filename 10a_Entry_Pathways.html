Apprentices & Trades

We're actively hiring for roles like Apprentice Boilermaker and Apprentice HV Mechanic to join our critical infrastructure team. You'll learn on the tools, earn real qualifications, and build a career that lasts.

  • Apprentice Boilermaker — fuel infrastructure fabrication
  • Apprentice HV Mechanic — heavy vehicle fleet maintenance
  • Structured on-the-job training with experienced mentors
  • Nationally recognised qualifications
  • Pathway to full-time employment at IOR

Graduates & Interns

Start your corporate or tech career in our entrepreneurial environment. We have pathways for graduates and interns across three key streams — and we move fast enough that you'll have real responsibility from day one.

  • Technology — help develop proprietary tech like HyDip®
  • Shared Services — finance, marketing, legal, commercial, P&C
  • Operations Support — ground fuels, aviation, supply chain
  • Mentoring from senior IOR leaders
  • Clear career path framework with digital tracking

School-Based & Trainees

Get a head start on your career while still at school. Our school-based programs give young Australians real industry experience and a clear pathway to full-time employment at IOR.

  • Earn while you learn — paid work experience
  • Nationally recognised training units
  • Available in regional and metro locations
  • Pathway to apprenticeship or full-time role
  • Supportive mentoring from day one

Working Regionally

IOR was born in the bush — the Inland Oil Refinery has operated out of Eromanga, QLD since 1984. Our entrepreneurial spirit has always been regional. Could you be part of the team that opens our next depot?

  • Roles across QLD, NSW, VIC, SA, WA, NT
  • Local employment programs in regional communities
  • Brand new Kenworth or Volvo for drivers
  • Career growth without leaving the community you love
  • LifeFlight, AgForce, and community sponsorships
