YOUR APPRENTICESHIP JOURNEY

Easy as 1, 2, 3

From application to qualified tradesperson — here's how your IOR apprenticeship works.

01

Apply & Get Selected

Submit your application via SEEK or careers@ior.com.au. A real person from our Talent Team reviews every application. If you're a good fit, we'll be in touch within 2 weeks.

02

Start Learning on the Tools

Your 2-day induction kicks off your journey. You'll be placed with an experienced mentor, working on real infrastructure — fuel tanks, heavy vehicles, and site equipment — from day one.

03

Qualify & Progress

Complete your nationally recognised qualification and step into a full-time role at IOR. Many of our senior technicians started exactly where you are now. This is how careers are built here.
