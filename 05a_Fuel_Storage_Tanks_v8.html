Bulk Fuel Tanks — T13 to T100

Our self-bunded bulk fuel tanks are available in sizes from 13,000L (T13) to 100,000L (T100), giving you the flexibility to right-size your storage for any operation. Fully portable with forklift pockets or lifting points, and manufactured from new steel to Australian Standards AS1692 and AS1940.

  • Available in 13KL, 22KL, 30KL, 50KL, 65KL, 80KL, and 100KL capacities
  • Manufactured from new steel — AS1692 & AS1940 compliant
  • Fully portable: forklift pockets and/or lifting points standard
  • Integral bunding — no secondary containment required
  • Lockable pump bay with integral dispensing equipment
  • Overfill alarm, venting, dipstick, and anti-syphon valve standard
  • Available with solar panel mounting for off-grid deployment

Combination Diesel & AdBlue® Tanks

For fleets running both diesel and AdBlue®, our combination tanks provide a single, compact storage solution with fully separate self-bunded compartments for each product. Available in a range of configurations to suit small to large operations.

  • TC14/03 — 13,775L Diesel / 2,375L AdBlue®
  • TC22/04 — 21,500L Diesel / 4,000L AdBlue®
  • TC30/05 — 29,000L Diesel / 5,000L AdBlue®
  • TC50/10 — 49,000L Diesel / 10,000L AdBlue®
  • TC65/12 — 63,500L Diesel / 12,000L AdBlue®
  • TC80/15 — 79,400L Diesel / 15,000L AdBlue®
  • Separate dispensing pumps and lockable bays for each product
  • Fully compliant with AS1692 & AS1940

Smart Features & Configuration Options

Every IOR tank is engineered with operational reliability in mind. Standard safety and monitoring features are included as standard, with a range of optional configurations for off-grid and remote deployments.

  • Lockable integral pump bay — prevents unauthorised access
  • High-level overfill alarm — audible and visual alert
  • Pressure/vacuum venting — compliant with AS1940
  • Calibrated dipstick — accurate manual level reading
  • Anti-syphon valve — prevents fuel theft and contamination
  • Solar panel mounting rails — for off-grid power supply
  • Configurable with 240V or 12/24V DC dispensing pumps
  • Optional flow meters, fuel management keypads, and hose reels
