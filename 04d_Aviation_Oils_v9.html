PRODUCT RANGE

The right lubricant for every aviation application.

From piston engine oils for general aviation to turbine oils for commercial and regional operators. IOR stocks a comprehensive range of aviation-grade lubricants to specification.

Piston Engine Oils

Ashless dispersant (AD) and straight mineral oils for piston-engine aircraft. Formulated to protect against wear, corrosion, and deposit formation in air-cooled aircraft engines.

  • Ashless dispersant (AD) grades — AeroShell W100, W100 Plus
  • Straight mineral oils for engine break-in and older aircraft
  • Multi-grade options for improved cold-start performance
Batch certificates and SDS provided as standard

Turbine Engine Oils

Synthetic turbine engine oils for turboprop, turbofan, and turboshaft applications. Formulated for high-temperature stability, load-carrying capacity, and corrosion protection in demanding turbine environments.

  • Type I and Type II synthetic turbine oils
  • AeroShell Turbine Oil 500, 560 series
  • OEM-approved grades for major turbine manufacturers
OEM approvals confirmed — contact us for your aircraft type

Aviation Greases

Specialised aviation greases for airframe bearings, wheel bearings, control surface hinges, and landing gear components. Formulated to withstand extreme temperature ranges and high-load conditions in aviation applications.

  • Airframe and wheel bearing greases (MIL-PRF-23827)
  • High-temperature greases for landing gear and control surfaces
  • AeroShell Grease 6, 7, 14, 22 series
Military and civil aviation specification grades available

Hydraulic Fluids (Aviation Spec)

Aviation-specification hydraulic fluids for aircraft flight control systems, landing gear, and braking systems. Mineral and synthetic formulations to meet civil and military aviation specifications.

  • Mineral hydraulic fluid (MIL-PRF-5606 / DEF STAN 91-048)
  • Phosphate ester fire-resistant fluid (Skydrol equivalent)
  • AeroShell Fluid 41, 31 series
Civil and military aviation specification grades stocked

SOVEREIGN SUPPLY CHAIN

One delivery. Fuel and lubricants together.

IOR maintains a sovereign supply chain backed by our own strategic terminal network. By leveraging this infrastructure, we co-deliver your aviation lubricants alongside your bulk Jet A-1 or Avgas order.

No separate supplier, no separate invoice, and no separate delivery coordination. Every lubricant delivery includes a batch certificate confirming product specification and a Safety Data Sheet (SDS). Documentation is provided as standard, not on request.
