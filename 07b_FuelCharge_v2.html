Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fuelcharge different from an IOR account?
Yes. Fuelcharge is a pay-as-you-go product — no credit account, no contract, no minimum spend. An IOR account gives you credit terms, volume pricing, and access to the full Customer Portal. You can upgrade from Fuelcharge to a full account at any time.
Where is Fuelcharge accepted?
Fuelcharge is accepted at 110+ IOR Diesel Network sites across Australia, including remote highway stops. Use the app to find your nearest site and check fuel availability before you travel.
What fuel types does Fuelcharge cover?
Fuelcharge covers diesel and AdBlue at IOR Diesel Network sites, and Avgas 100LL and Jet A-1 at IOR Aviation Network sites where available.
Is there a Fuelcharge card, or is it app-only?
Fuelcharge is available as both an app and a physical card. The app is the recommended option — it includes the site finder, digital receipts, and spend tracking. Contact us to request a physical Fuelcharge card.
Are there any fees?
There are no account fees or subscription fees for Fuelcharge. You pay only for the fuel you purchase. Standard card payment surcharges may apply at some sites.
