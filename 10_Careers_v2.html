THE PERKS OF LIVING BLUE

Working at IOR is more than a job

We're ambitious, growing fast, and looking for people who want to get stuck in. In exchange, we'll back you with the right tools, the right team, and real opportunity.

For Drivers

Get behind the wheel of a brand new Kenworth or Volvo you'll call your own, with support from a dedicated Driver Experience team. Your truck, your route, your career.

For Tradies

Great rates and varied work — we do our own engine repairs in-house, so you're not doing the same thing every day. Real skills, real variety, real progression.

For Aviation Crew

No day is the same in our fastest-growing business unit. We're on a mission to expand our international aviation network — and we need sharp people to help lead it.

For Corporate & Admin

Even from behind the scenes, you'll see the real-world impact of your work — whether it's keeping a remote community powered or getting a LifeFlight helicopter in the air.

Oh, and you'll also get staff and family discounts on fuel — a pretty handy perk right now.

OUR VALUES

Not words on a wall

At IOR, our values aren't just words on a wall. They are the principles that guide our actions, decisions, and relationships every day.

Innovation: Finding Solutions

  • We work hard to understand our customers and our team
  • We challenge ourselves to improve every time
  • We experiment and find creative solutions

Our Communities: Serving People

  • We make sure our team is respected, safe, and engaged
  • We grow together with customers and suppliers
  • We take responsibility for our communities' success

Reliability: Getting Results

  • We communicate early, clearly and directly
  • We do what we say
  • We always find a way
