# IOR Brand Guidelines

## 1. Core Identity
IOR is Australia's sovereign commercial fuel infrastructure company. From refinery to pump, IOR owns the supply chain. The brand must reflect reliability, scale, and deep connection to regional Australia.

## 2. Tone of Voice
- **Direct & Confident:** Speak with authority. Avoid corporate fluff and marketing jargon.
- **Regional Australian:** Grounded, practical, and respectful of the hard work done in the bush, on the road, and in the air.
- **Safety First:** "Zero Harm. No compromise." Safety is non-negotiable and should be treated with absolute seriousness.
- **Partnership:** IOR doesn't just sell fuel; it fuels the industries that keep Australia moving.

## 3. Colour Palette
The colour palette is strictly controlled. Do not introduce new shades or opacities outside of the design system.

| Colour Name | Hex Code | CSS Token | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary Blue** | `#0355A3` | `--ior-blue` | Primary buttons, active states, key highlights |
| **Navy** | `#0A4066` | `--ior-navy` | Secondary backgrounds, headers |
| **Deep Navy** | `#0B1929` | `--ior-deep` | Main footer, dark section backgrounds |
| **Sky Blue** | `#4DA8DA` | `--ior-sky` | Accents, hover states, secondary links |
| **Aviation Gold** | `#C9A227` | `--av-gold` | Strictly reserved for Aviation division accents |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | `--ior-white` | Text on dark backgrounds, card backgrounds |
| **Off-White** | `#F5F7FA` | `--off-white` | Subtle section backgrounds |

## 4. Typography
- **Primary Font:** `Inter` (sans-serif).
- **Hierarchy:** Clear distinction between `h1` (hero), `h2` (section titles), `h3` (card titles), and `p` (body copy).
- **Weight:** Use `700` (Bold) or `900` (Black) for headings, `400` (Regular) for body copy.

## 5. Hard Rules
1. **No Rounded Corners:** `border-radius` must be `0` on all UI elements (cards, buttons, images, inputs). The brand is industrial and sharp.
2. **No "Depot":** The word "depot" is strictly forbidden. Use "site", "office", or "location" depending on context.
3. **Logo Placeholder:** Until the final SVG is provided, the logo must be represented exactly as `[IOR LOGO]` in text.
4. **Photography:** Images must be full-bleed where possible, often with a dark overlay (`rgba(11, 25, 41, 0.7)`) to ensure text readability.
