OUR STORY

Fuel for the Outback

IOR is different. While many companies start on the coast and work their way inland, we did the reverse. We were born in the bush, with the Inland Oil Refinery operating out of Eromanga, QLD, since 1984. Our entrepreneurial spirit has always been regional.

We've always been passionate about delivering 'Fuel for the Outback'. As we've grown and evolved, that passion has never left us. Could you be part of the team that opens our next site?

COMMUNITY INVESTMENT

Part of the community

When we set up a site or unmanned fuel site in a new regional town, it's never just bricks and mortar. We invest heavily in the communities we serve — from local employment to sponsoring LifeFlight, AgForce, and regional sporting clubs.

Our local employment program seeks to attract people from within the communities we serve. Your work with us has a real, tangible impact. What starts in your regional town goes on to power our nation.
