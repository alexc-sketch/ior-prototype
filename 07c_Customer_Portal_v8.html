THE PROBLEM

Fuel management shouldn’t require a spreadsheet

Most businesses track fuel consumption manually — across multiple spreadsheets, paper dockets, and phone calls. It’s slow, error-prone, and leaves you exposed to fraud. The IOR Customer Portal changes that entirely.

  • No real-time visibility into fuel stock or usage
  • Manual reconciliation of invoices and dockets
  • No way to detect or prevent unauthorised draws
  • Tag and driver management done over the phone
  • Fuel Tax Credit claims that take hours to compile

FEATURES

Everything you need, out of the box

The Customer Portal comes fully configured the moment your account is activated. No extra setup, no additional cost — just log in and take control.

Fuel Visibility & Security

See your real-time fuel stock, consumption history, and delivery records in one dashboard. Know exactly what’s in your tanks at any moment — from any device.

Fraud Prevention

Instantly suspend or reactivate RFID tags from the portal. Set spend limits, restrict fuel types, and receive alerts on unusual activity — protecting your fleet around the clock.

Optimise Fuel Usage

Detailed consumption reports by vehicle, driver, or site help you identify inefficiencies and reduce waste. Export ATO-compliant data for Fuel Tax Credit claims in seconds.

Forward Planning

Forecast fuel needs based on historical usage patterns. Schedule deliveries, set low-stock alerts, and integrate with HyDip™ FMS for automated reordering — so you never run dry.
