Flight Training Adelaide: Fuelling the Next Generation of Pilots

Client: Flight Training Adelaide (FTA) / Wagner Corporation

When the Qantas Group Pilot Training Academy opened at Toowoomba Wellcamp Airport, IOR built a state-of-the-art refuelling facility capable of fuelling three aircraft simultaneously.

The Challenge

In January 2020, Qantas Group CEO Alan Joyce and Queensland Premier Annastacia Palaszczuk opened the Qantas Group Pilot Training Academy at Toowoomba's Wellcamp International Airport. The new purpose-built facility required a state-of-the-art refuelling solution capable of supporting high-frequency training operations — fuelling multiple aircraft simultaneously, reliably, and to the highest aviation fuel quality standards.

Flight Training Adelaide (FTA), appointed as the training provider for the Toowoomba site, needed a fuel partner who could design, build, and operate a refuelling facility that matched the ambition of the project — and IOR's long-standing commitment to Toowoomba and South-West Queensland made them the natural choice.

The Solution

IOR drew on over a decade of aviation infrastructure experience to develop a unique customer solution for the Toowoomba Wellcamp Airport. The refuelling facility is capable of fuelling three aircraft simultaneously from a 55,000-litre tank — meeting the high-tempo demands of a world-class pilot training academy.

  • 55,000-litre aviation fuel storage tank designed and installed
  • Multi-point refuelling system capable of fuelling three aircraft simultaneously
  • Full compliance with CASA aviation fuel quality and safety standards
  • Ongoing Jet A-1 fuel supply to support daily training operations
  • Dedicated IOR aviation account management for FTA and Wagner Corporation

The project extended IOR's long-standing commitment to Toowoomba and South-West Queensland, and demonstrated IOR's entrepreneurial and innovative capability to meet the unique requirements of Australia's aviation training sector.

"IOR demonstrated its entrepreneurial and innovative capability to meet our needs at the Toowoomba Wellcamp Airport facility. Their commitment to regional Queensland is genuine."
— Flight Training Adelaide (FTA), 2020
