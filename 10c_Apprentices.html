About the Program

A real trade. A real future.

IOR's apprenticeship program gives you the opportunity to learn a trade in a real-world environment. You'll work alongside experienced tradespeople, gain hands-on experience with fuel infrastructure, and earn a nationally recognised qualification.

We offer apprenticeships in areas including mechanical, electrical, and instrumentation trades — all critical to keeping Australia's fuel network running.

At IOR, apprentices are valued members of the team from day one. You'll be mentored, supported, and given every opportunity to grow.

What You Get

Why apprentice with IOR?

💰
Competitive Pay
Earn a real wage from day one, with pay that increases as you progress through your qualification.
🎓
Nationally Recognised Qualification
Complete a Certificate III or IV in your chosen trade — a qualification that opens doors across Australia.
🤝
Mentorship & Support
You'll be paired with an experienced mentor who will guide you through every stage of your apprenticeship.
🚀
Career Pathway
Many of our senior tradespeople started as apprentices. We promote from within and invest in our people's long-term growth.
📍
Work Across Australia
Gain experience at multiple IOR sites across Australia — from our Brisbane HQ to remote outback locations.
🛡️
Safety First
IOR's zero-harm culture means you'll learn to work safely from day one — a skill that will serve you for life.
How to Apply

Three simple steps.

1
Express Your Interest

2
Meet the Team

3
Start Your Journey
Once you're on board, you'll be set up with your training plan, your mentor, and everything you need to hit the ground running.
