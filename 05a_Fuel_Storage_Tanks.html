Frequently Asked Questions

Fuel Storage Tank FAQs

What is the minimum order size for a fuel storage tank?
IOR supplies fuel storage tanks from 1,000L. The right size depends on your daily fuel consumption, delivery frequency, and site access. Our specialists can help you determine the optimal tank size for your operation.
Do I need council approval to install a fuel storage tank?
Requirements vary by state, territory, and local council. IOR manages all permit applications and EPA approvals as part of our turnkey installation service. We know the requirements in every jurisdiction we operate in.
What is the difference between a single-skin and bunded tank?
A single-skin tank has one wall and requires an external bund (containment area) to meet AS 1940 requirements. A bunded (double-wall) tank has an integrated secondary containment layer built in — no external bund required. Bunded tanks are preferred for environmental compliance and ease of installation.
Can IOR install a tank at a remote location?
Yes. IOR has extensive experience installing fuel storage infrastructure at remote mining, agricultural, and construction sites across Australia. We manage all logistics including transport, crane hire, and civil works.
