Hire or Buy — what's right for you?

IOR offers both hire and purchase options for frac tanks. The right choice depends on your operation's duration, budget, and future needs.

Hire

Frac Tank Hire

Ideal for temporary operations, seasonal fuel needs, or when you need capacity fast without capital outlay.

  • No capital expenditure required
  • Flexible hire terms (weekly, monthly, project-based)
  • IOR handles delivery, setup, and collection
  • Maintenance and compliance included
  • Scale up or down as your needs change
  • Ideal for: harvest season, construction projects, mining exploration
Purchase

Frac Tank Purchase

Ideal for long-term or permanent deployments where ownership makes financial sense over time.

  • Asset ownership — no ongoing hire costs
  • Customise to your exact specifications
  • HyDip™ FMS integration available
  • IOR can maintain under a service agreement
  • Depreciation benefits for eligible businesses
  • Ideal for: remote stations, permanent mine sites, long-term projects

BLOCK 8 — FAQs · Frac Tanks specific
Frequently Asked Questions

Frac Tank FAQs

How quickly can IOR deploy a frac tank?
IOR can typically deploy frac tanks within 3–7 business days to most locations. For urgent requirements, contact us directly on 1300 457 467 — we'll do our best to expedite delivery.
What site preparation is required for a frac tank?
No civil works are required. You need a level, stable surface capable of supporting the tank's weight when full. Gravel or compacted earth is suitable. IOR will advise on specific requirements for your site during the enquiry process.
What is the minimum hire period?
Minimum hire periods vary by tank size and location. Contact us for current minimum hire terms and rates. We offer weekly, monthly, and project-based hire arrangements.
Can IOR supply fuel directly to the frac tank?
Yes. IOR can supply fuel directly to your frac tank as part of a combined equipment and supply arrangement. This simplifies your logistics — one supplier for both the tank and the fuel.
Are IOR frac tanks compliant with environmental regulations?
Yes. All IOR frac tanks are bunded (integrated secondary containment) to meet environmental compliance requirements in all Australian states and territories. IOR can provide compliance documentation on request.
