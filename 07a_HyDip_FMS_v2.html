The problem with manual fuel management

Manual dip readings are time-consuming, error-prone, and leave you blind between checks. Runouts cost you downtime. Overfills cost you money. Compliance gaps cost you audits. There's a better way.

HyDip™ FMS: your always-on fuel intelligence

HyDip™ FMS continuously monitors your tanks and sends real-time data to your dashboard and phone. You see every site, every tank, every litre — without leaving your desk.

What HyDip™ FMS Does

Four components. One complete system.

HyDip™ FMS is built around four integrated components that work together to give you complete fuel visibility.

Automated tank monitoring

HyDip™ sensors continuously measure fuel levels in your tanks and transmit readings to your dashboard in real time. No manual dips. No guesswork.

Continuous level monitoring — readings every 15 minutes
Compatible with diesel, petrol, and AdBlue tanks
Multi-site dashboard — all tanks in one view
Historical trend data for forecasting and ordering
Works on remote sites with satellite connectivity

Alerts & notifications

Set custom thresholds and receive instant alerts via SMS or email when tanks reach low-stock levels, anomalies are detected, or deliveries are confirmed.

Low-stock alerts — set your own thresholds
Leak and anomaly detection alerts
Delivery confirmation notifications
SMS and email delivery to multiple contacts
Escalation rules for unacknowledged alerts

Generate audit-ready reports in seconds. HyDip™ FMS captures every dip reading, delivery, and anomaly — giving you a complete paper trail for compliance and insurance purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What tank types is HyDip™ FMS compatible with?
HyDip™ FMS is compatible with above-ground and below-ground tanks for diesel, petrol, and AdBlue. Our team will assess your tank configuration during the enquiry process.
How long does installation take?
Most single-site installations are completed within one business day. Multi-site rollouts are scheduled in consultation with your operations team.
Does HyDip™ FMS work on remote sites without reliable internet?
Yes. HyDip™ FMS supports satellite connectivity for remote sites where mobile or fixed-line internet is unavailable. Our team will recommend the right connectivity solution for your location.
Is HyDip™ FMS only available to IOR customers?
HyDip™ FMS is available to IOR fuel supply customers. If you're not yet an IOR customer, you can apply for an account as part of the enquiry process.
What does the HyDip™ FMS subscription include?
Your subscription includes hardware, installation, ongoing monitoring, software updates, and access to the HyDip™ dashboard and IOR Customer Portal. Contact our team for pricing details.
