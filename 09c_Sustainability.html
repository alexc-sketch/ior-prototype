Our Commitment

Safety is not a department. It's a culture.

At IOR, we are committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all our employees, contractors, and visitors. Our Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) framework is built on the principle of zero harm — to people, communities, and the environment.

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of environmental stewardship. From our Eromanga Refinery to our national network of unmanned sites, every operation is designed to minimise risk and protect the places we work in.

Our ESG Framework

Environmental. Social. Governance.

E
Environmental
We manage our environmental footprint through rigorous monitoring, spill prevention protocols, and investment in cleaner operations. Our Eromanga Refinery and terminal infrastructure are operated to minimise emissions and environmental impact.
S
Social
We invest in the communities we operate in through sponsorships, local employment programs, and our buy local policy. We are committed to equal opportunity and are developing our Reconciliation Action Plan.
G
Governance
We operate with full transparency and accountability. Our governance framework includes clear policies, audit trails, and a commitment to ethical business conduct across every level of the organisation.
HSE Framework

How we keep people and places safe.

🛡️
Safety Management System
Our Safety Management System is aligned to ISO 45001 and provides a structured framework for identifying, assessing, and controlling workplace hazards across all IOR operations.
🌿
Environmental Management
We operate in accordance with ISO 14001 environmental management principles. Our sites are designed to prevent spills, manage waste, and protect local ecosystems.
📋
Incident Reporting & Audit
All incidents are reported, investigated, and actioned. We maintain full audit trails across our operations, with regular internal and third-party audits to verify compliance and drive continuous improvement.
🎓
Training & Competency
Every IOR team member receives role-specific HSE training. We invest in ongoing competency development to ensure our people have the knowledge and skills to work safely in every environment.
