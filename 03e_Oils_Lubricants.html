Product Range

The complete heavy-duty lubricant range

🔧

Engine Oils

Heavy-duty diesel engine oils for on-highway and off-highway applications. API CK-4 and FA-4 compliant. OEM-approved for Cummins, CAT, Volvo, and Mercedes-Benz.

💧

Hydraulic Fluids

Anti-wear hydraulic oils for mobile and stationary equipment. ISO VG 32, 46, 68, and 100 grades. Suitable for high-pressure systems in mining and construction equipment.

⚙️

Transmission Fluids

Automatic and manual transmission fluids for heavy-duty drivetrains. Allison TES-295 and Caterpillar TO-4 approved grades available.

🛢️

Greases

Lithium complex and calcium sulfonate greases for chassis, wheel bearings, and open gear applications. NLGI grades 1, 2, and 3 available.

🌡️

Coolants & Antifreeze

OAT and HOAT extended-life coolants for diesel engines. Pre-mixed and concentrate formats. Suitable for extreme temperature environments.

🧴

Specialty Fluids

Brake fluids, power steering fluids, and compressor oils for specialised applications. Available in drum, IBC, and bulk formats.
