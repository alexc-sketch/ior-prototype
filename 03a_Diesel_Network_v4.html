Why the IOR Diesel Network

Engineered for heavy transport.

High-Flow Diesel

Get back on the road faster. Ultra high-flow, fast-fill bowsers designed specifically for heavy combination vehicles.

HyBlue™ AdBlue®

ISO-certified AdBlue® available directly at the pump at select stops, eliminating the need for packaged plastic bladders.

B-Double & Quad Access

Sweeping canopies and massive turning circles at approved sites. No reversing, no unhooking trailers, no tight squeezes.

Driver Amenities

Clean, secure facilities. Select locations feature driver amenities including high-pressure truck washes, showers, and toilets.

FAQs

IOR Diesel Network — common questions.

  • The IOR Diesel Network is Australia’s largest unmanned commercial diesel network, with 115+ locations across major transport corridors. Drivers use an RFID tag to fuel 24/7 without cash or receipts. All transactions are consolidated into a single monthly invoice.
  • Any business with a registered IOR account can access the Diesel Network. It is particularly suited to transport operators, fleet managers, and owner-drivers running regular interstate or regional routes. Mining, civil, and agricultural operators also use the network for over-the-road fuelling.
  • Use the IOR Location Finder to search by state, corridor, or postcode. The full IOR Diesel Network Directory (downloadable above) lists all locations with GPS coordinates, facilities, and access details.
  • Yes. The network is designed for fleet operations. Each driver receives an individual RFID tag linked to your account. All transactions are reported per driver and per vehicle, giving fleet managers full visibility of fuel spend without managing receipts or reimbursements.
  • Yes. The IOR Diesel Network includes stops in regional and remote areas along major transport corridors, including locations not serviced by traditional service stations. The network is specifically designed to support long-haul and regional operations.
  • Apply for an IOR account using the button on this page. Once approved, RFID tags are dispatched promptly. There are no hidden establishment or maintenance fees — you pay only for fuel consumed.
