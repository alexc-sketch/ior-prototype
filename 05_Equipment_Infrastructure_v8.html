The IOR Ecosystem

Hardware meets digital visibility.

A storage tank is just steel without the right technology. All IOR self-bunded tanks are deployed HyDip™ ready.

Connect your physical infrastructure to our proprietary Fuel Management System to track every drop of fuel across your entire project in real time. Eliminate manual dipping, prevent run-outs, and automate your procurement.

  • Live tank level monitoring from any device
  • Automated reorder triggers directly to IOR dispatch
  • Equipment and driver-level transaction reporting
  • Real-time anomaly and theft alerts

Why IOR

We build the infrastructure we rely on ourselves.

IOR does not just sell tanks. We operate a national network of over 115 unmanned diesel stops and manage remote supply chains across Australia. The self-bunded tanks and fluid storage solutions we deploy for our clients are the exact same assets we trust to keep our own business running.

When you partner with IOR, you get infrastructure proven in the field.

  • Field-tested across IOR's own national diesel network
  • Turnkey deployment includes tank delivery, installation, and fuel supply
  • Fully compliant with Australian Standards (AS1692 & AS1940)
  • Integrated HyDip™ telemetry for real-time tank monitoring

Equipment & Infrastructure: Common Questions.

IOR offers both flexible hire agreements and direct purchase options for our self-bunded tanks and frac tanks. We can structure a commercial agreement that best suits your project timeline and capital expenditure requirements.

Our standard self-bunded storage tanks range from 13,000 litres (T13) up to 100,000 litres (T100). We can also link multiple tanks together to create a high-capacity modular tank farm for major projects.

Yes. All IOR fuel storage tanks are fully compliant with Australian Standards, including AS1940 (The storage and handling of flammable and combustible liquids) and AS1692 (Steel tanks for flammable and combustible liquids).

Our tanks come HyDip™ ready for seamless integration with our proprietary Fuel Management System. However, our infrastructure can be adapted to work with most major third-party fuel management systems if required.
