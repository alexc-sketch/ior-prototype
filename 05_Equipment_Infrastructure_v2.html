Getting Started

Infrastructure deployed in 5 business days

From enquiry to operational infrastructure — faster than you think.

1

Submit an Infrastructure Enquiry

Tell us your site location, fuel type, volume requirements, and timeline. IOR responds within 24 hours.

2

Site Assessment & Proposal

IOR's infrastructure team visits your site and provides a detailed proposal including tank sizing, placement, and HyDip™ monitoring configuration.

3

Installation & Commissioning

IOR delivers, installs, and commissions the infrastructure. AS1940 compliance documentation is provided on handover.
