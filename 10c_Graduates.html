GRADUATE PROGRAM

Three streams. One goal: go far.

IMAGE PLACEHOLDER
IOR Graduate team in office
Corporate setting, IOR branded, collaborative
STREAM 1

Technology

Help develop our proprietary technology and solutions, including HyDip® — our fuel management system used across Australia's mining, transport, and agriculture sectors.

  • Software development and product management
  • Data analytics and business intelligence
  • Cybersecurity and infrastructure
  • Work directly with our CTO and tech leadership
STREAM 2

Shared Services

Find your fit in finance, marketing, legal, commercial, or people and culture. You'll see the full picture of a growing Australian energy business from the inside.

  • Finance and commercial analysis
  • Marketing and brand
  • Legal and compliance
  • People and culture
STREAM 3

Operations Support

Learn the core of our business — supporting our ground fuels, aviation, or supply chain teams. This is where you understand how IOR actually works, from terminal to tank.

  • Ground fuels operations and logistics
  • Aviation network coordination
  • Supply chain and terminal operations
  • Infrastructure and site management
