Getting Started

Airport fuelling partnership in 5 steps

From initial discussion to first fuelling operation.

1

Initial Consultation

IOR's aviation team meets with your airport operations team to understand requirements, volumes, and compliance needs.

2

Site Assessment & Proposal

IOR conducts a site assessment and provides a detailed proposal covering operations, pricing, and SLA commitments.

3

Compliance & Certification

IOR's fuelling crews complete JIG training and site-specific induction. All documentation is provided.

4

Infrastructure Setup

IOR installs or integrates with existing fuelling infrastructure. Hydrant systems, refuellers, and storage tanks.

5

Operations Commence

IOR manages all fuelling operations. You receive consolidated invoicing and full compliance documentation.

We own the infrastructure.
