Who We Are

We are more than fuel.

IOR is an Australian operated business, dedicated to service and committed to local, hands-on relationships with our customers and suppliers. We are passionate about making sure our hard-working customers across Australia can fuel up, keep going, and keep growing.

We own every part of our supply chain. From our inland refinery in Eromanga to our proprietary HyDip® Fuel Management System, we invest in the infrastructure that keeps Australia moving.

Our History

40 years of fuelling Australia.

1984
Eromanga Refinery begins operations. IOR is born in the Outback.
2005
First unmanned 24/7 diesel stop opens in Winton, QLD.
2014
IOR enters aviation fuels market.
2016
Port Bonython Terminal commissioned. 81ML capacity.
2023
Lytton Terminal opens. Largest diesel-only terminal in Queensland.
2025
115+ unmanned sites. 190M+ litres delivered annually.
