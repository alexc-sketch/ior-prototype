Getting Started

Mine site fuel supply in 5 business days

Tell us your site location and we'll have infrastructure on the ground within a week.

1

Submit an Enquiry

Contact IOR's mining supply team. Tell us your site location, fuel types, volumes, and project timeline. We respond within 4 hours.

2

Site Assessment & Logistics Plan

IOR's logistics team develops a supply plan. Frac tank deployment, delivery schedule, HyDip™ monitoring, and HSE documentation.

3

Infrastructure Deployment & Commissioning

IOR delivers and installs frac tanks and storage infrastructure. AS1940 compliance documentation provided on handover.
