Service Options

Four ways IOR delivers fuel to your site

From scheduled site fills to emergency on-call refuelling — IOR has a managed solution for every operation.

UX COMPONENT: Sticky-scroll Options 1–4 Each option card sticks as user scrolls through. Approved layout per previous feedback round.
1️⃣

Scheduled Site Refuelling

IOR's X2U refueller visits your site on a pre-agreed schedule — daily, weekly, or fortnightly. Machines are fuelled during shift changes or overnight to eliminate production downtime.

MiningConstructionAgriculture
2️⃣

On-Call Emergency Refuelling

Machine runs dry mid-shift? IOR dispatches a refueller to your site within the agreed SLA. No production shutdown. No waiting for a scheduled drop.

MiningCivil
3️⃣

Site Tank Management

IOR installs and manages on-site storage tanks. HyDip™ FMS monitors levels remotely and triggers automatic reorders before you run low. Zero manual intervention required.

AgricultureTransport
4️⃣

Integrated Fleet Fuelling

Combine on-site refuelling with IOR Diesel Network access for drivers on the road. One account, one invoice, full visibility across your entire fuel spend.

Getting Started

Site assessment to first fill in 5 business days

1

Book a Site Assessment

2

Receive Your Tailored Proposal
