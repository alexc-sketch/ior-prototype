Why IOR Aviation

Built for aviation's uncompromising standards.

IOR's aviation division operates to the strictest quality control standards in the industry. Our dedicated aviation tanker fleet never carries ground fuels — eliminating the contamination and switch loading risks that can ground an aircraft or compromise engine performance.

From terminal to wingtip, every step of our fuel handling process is governed by JIG (Joint Inspection Group) standards and independently verified. We partner with Australia's largest fuel importers to ensure our Jet A-1 and Avgas meets specification at every delivery point.

Compliance & Quality

The standards that protect your aircraft.

Aviation buyers demand compliance above all else. Every IOR aviation operation is governed by the industry's most stringent standards.

JIG Compliant Operations

All IOR airport fuelling operations are conducted in strict accordance with Joint Inspection Group (JIG) standards — the global benchmark for aviation fuel quality and safety. Our trained personnel are JIG-certified and regularly audited.

JIG Certified

ISO Certified Quality

IOR's quality management systems are ISO certified, ensuring consistent, documented processes across every aviation fuel delivery. Batch certificates and Safety Data Sheets are provided with every delivery as standard.

ISO Certified

Dedicated Aviation Infrastructure

Our aviation tanker fleet is exclusively dedicated to aviation fuels — never used for ground fuels. This eliminates switch loading risk and ensures Jet A-1 and Avgas purity from our terminal to your aircraft.

Zero Cross-Contamination

Frequently asked questions

IOR supplies Jet A-1 (kerosene-based turbine fuel), Avgas 100LL (low-lead piston engine fuel), and UL91 (unleaded piston engine fuel) across our national aviation network. We also supply aviation-grade oils and lubricants including AeroShell and ExxonMobil Aviation products, co-delivered with your bulk fuel order.

Yes. All IOR airport fuelling operations are conducted in strict accordance with Joint Inspection Group (JIG) standards — the global benchmark for aviation fuel quality and safety. Our trained personnel are JIG-certified and regularly audited. Batch certificates and Safety Data Sheets are provided with every delivery as standard.

Yes. IOR's aviation tanker fleet is exclusively dedicated to aviation fuels and is never used to carry ground fuels such as diesel. This eliminates switch loading risk and ensures the purity of Jet A-1 and Avgas from our terminal to your aircraft. This is a critical distinction — and a key reason why aviation operators choose IOR.

Yes. IOR's national fleet is specifically built for remote and regional delivery. We regularly supply Jet A-1 and Avgas to mining camp airstrips, remote pastoral properties, and regional airports across Queensland, Western Australia, the Northern Territory, and beyond. Contact our aviation team to discuss your location and requirements.

IOR self-serve aviation sites are accessible 24/7 via the Fuelcharge app. Simply scan the QR code at the bowser, select your fuel type, and pay via the app — no account or credit check required. For IOR account holders, fuel can also be accessed using your IOR card. Visit the Aviation Network page for a full list of self-serve locations.

IOR provides batch certificates (Certificate of Analysis) and Safety Data Sheets (SDS) with every aviation fuel delivery as standard. For into-plane fuelling operations, our personnel maintain full JIG-compliant documentation including fuelling records, filter checks, and water detection logs. All documentation is available digitally via the IOR Customer Portal.
